The details

The story
Bordeaux is our reading of a modern icon — masterfully reimagined and made the عطّار way, on rich oils rather than alcohol, so it stays true to the original and lasts far longer. Blended in small batches in Egypt and poured to order.
How to wear it
Spray on pulse points — wrists, neck, and once on the fabric of your scarf or collar, where oil-based scent lingers for days. Two sprays is plenty; this one carries.
Ingredients
Alcohol-free perfume oil base with saffron, benzaldehyde, jasmine absolute, cedarwood, amberwood accord, fir balsam, and agarwood (oud). Full INCI on the carton.
Shipping & returns
Ships from Egypt in 2–4 days. Because fragrance is personal, we can't accept opened returns — which is exactly why we offer the 5ml tester for LE 85: try it first, commit only when you love it.
