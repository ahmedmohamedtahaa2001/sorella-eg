Get To Know Us

At Sorella, we believe that the best bonds are those that are shared. We’re two sisters, connected not just by family, but by a shared love for fragrance and the memories they create. Our journey began with a simple desire to bring people closer together through the power of scent, and that’s how Sorella was born.

- 
- 

Why Sorella?

The name Sorella reflects our deep sisterly bond, and it’s our mission to share that connection with you. We’ve poured our hearts into each fragrance, carefully crafting scents that evoke emotions, create lasting memories, and celebrate the moments that make life special.

- 
- 

Fragrance With a Story

Our love for perfumes isn’t just about creating beautiful scents—it’s about telling stories and inspiring connections. Every fragrance is a reflection of the closeness we share and our hope that, through our perfumes, you’ll feel that same warmth and connection.
