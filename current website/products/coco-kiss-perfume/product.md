## Description

A soft, cozy gourmand that smells like a warm coconut dessert melting into your skin.

What you’ll smell:
It opens creamy and milky with a toasted coconut vibe, slightly nutty and smooth—not overly sweet. As it settles, it turns into a warm vanilla with a hint of buttery richness, almost like coconut cream with a soft woody touch in the background. It’s subtle, comforting, and very “your skin but better.”

Notes:

-
Top: Coconut cream

-
Heart: Toasted praline

-
Base: Vanilla, warm woods

Vibe:
Clean girl but cozy. Effortless, soft, and addictive—like warm skin after a beach shower.

When to wear:
Perfect for daytime, casual outings, gym, or layering with deeper vanillas at night. Best in spring/summer but honestly works year-round if you like soft gourmands.
