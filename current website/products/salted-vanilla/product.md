## Description

The ultimate addictive gourmand—sweet, warm, and instantly recognizable.

It opens with a rich pistachio and almond blend that feels nutty and slightly creamy. Then the salted caramel kicks in—sweet, buttery, and mouthwatering—balanced by a soft floral touch. As it settles, it melts into warm vanilla with a hint of sandalwood, leaving a delicious, beachy-sweet trail.

Notes:

Top: Pistachio, almond

-

-

Vibe:
Flirty, beachy, and confident. It’s that “hot girl on vacation” energy—fun, glowing, and attention-grabbing.
