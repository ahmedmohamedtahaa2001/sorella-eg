Rethinking the fragrance industry became a reality.
Dossier was founded to make premium fragrances and the world of French perfumery accessible to everyone. We saw how a lack of transparency and excessive markups plagued the fragrance industry. So, we planted our roots in Grasse and established our fair-priced perfumery. 
All Dossier fragrances are crafted in Grasse, France (the perfume capital of the world).
