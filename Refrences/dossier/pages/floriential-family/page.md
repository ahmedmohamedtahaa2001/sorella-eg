Florential Family

This family brings together perfumes that mix floral and oriental notes.
Oriental and floral raw materials perfectly fit together. Floral notes soften the oriental ones, while oriental notes warm up the floral ones.
A number of perfumes - almost exclusively feminine - are built on the balance between these two families, like Floriental Orange Blossom (inspired by Estée Lauder’s Beautiful), or more recently, Floriental Vanilla (inspired by YSL’s Black Opium) or Floriental Coconut (inspired by Tom Ford’s Soleil Blanc).
