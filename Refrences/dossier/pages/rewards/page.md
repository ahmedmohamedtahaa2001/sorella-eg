The Dossier Rewards Program has ended.
The following Terms and Conditions are applicable to Dossier Rewards Program members as of August 24, 2024. Effective August 24, 2024, the Dossier Rewards Program was cancelled and no new members or new loyalty points will be issued. Dossier Rewards Program members with at least 500 loyalty points in their Rewards Program Account as of August 24, 2024 may continue to maintain their Program account. If Program account contains less than 500 loyalty points at any time after the Ending Time and Date, we will automatically close your account and cancel all remaining loyalty points as noted above.
For customers who have not yet activated a Rewards Program account , you will have until September 24, 2024 to do so and take advantage of any loyalty points you may have available to you once an account is established. To open a Rewards Program account, please go here . If you elect not to activate a Rewards Program account, all loyalty points associated with your nonmember status will be be cancelled. If your Rewards Program account has less than 500 points in it as of the Ending Time and Date, it will be automatically closed and all remaining loyalty points will be cancelled as noted above.
For customers who are Dossier+ members , your Dossier+ credits and Dossier+ Perks will be unaffected. If you are also a Rewards Program member with a Rewards Program account, this change will only impact the loyalty points you may have accumulated in your Rewards Program account. You will continue to participate in the Dossier+ Program under its current terms.
If you have any questions about these changes, please reach out to help@dossier.co .
We thank you for your loyalty and appreciate your support and purchase of our products.
If you are not already a Dossier+ member, we hope you will consider joining our Dossier+ Program to take advantage of the perks of that program.
Dossier reserves the right to add, remove or modify special program benefits at any time, but we will use reasonable efforts to notify you of any material changes. You may always unsubscribe from a special program at any time.
