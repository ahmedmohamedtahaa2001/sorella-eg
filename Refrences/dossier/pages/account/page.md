The Dossier Rewards Program has ended.
The following Terms and Conditions are applicable to Dossier Rewards Program members as of August 24, 2024. Effective August 24, 2024, the Dossier Rewards Program was cancelled and no new memberships or loyalty points will be issued as of that date (“Ending Date”). Dossier Rewards Program members with at least 500 loyalty points in their Rewards Program Account as of August 24, 2024 may continue to maintain their Program account. If Program account contains less than 500 loyalty points at any time after the Ending Date, we will automatically close your account and all remaining loyalty points will no longer be redeemable.
Important: all unredeemed loyalty points in your Program account must be redeemed on or before December 31, 2024. Any loyalty points that have not been redeemed after that date will expire and will be no longer be redeemable.
Customers who had not activated a Rewards Program account as of the Ending Date , had until September 24, 2024 to do so. Any customer that elected not to activate a Rewards Program account after the Ending Date is no longer eligible to redeem any loyalty points.
For customers who are Dossier+ members , your Dossier+ credits and Dossier+ Perks will be unaffected. If you are also a Rewards Program member with a Rewards Program account, this change will only impact the unredeemed loyalty points you may have accumulated in your Rewards Program account. You will continue to participate in the Dossier+ Program under its current terms.
If you have any questions about these changes, please reach out to help@dossier.co .
We thank you for your loyalty and appreciate your support and purchase of our products.
If you are not already a Dossier+ member, we hope you will consider joining our Dossier+ Program to take advantage of the perks of that program.
Dossier reserves the right to add, remove or modify special program benefits at any time, but we will use reasonable efforts to notify you of any material changes. You may always unsubscribe from a special program at any time.
