ALLYIAH 

MEET N GREET RECAP 

Sep 11, 2025
Let's Recap: Meet & Greet With Allyiah Gainer! 
Attendees lined up around the block for this event at our NoLita boutique. Allyiah and the Dossier team hosted a spritz & chat soirée where attendees had the chance to purchase this otherwise out-of-stock fragrance duo (Sold out in under one day!) and the campaign's luxurious universe. Customers enjoyed exclusive photo opportunities with the influencer, complimentary perfume engraving services, tote bags with their purchase, and more.
Oh là là! This spritz and chat with Allyiah was scent-sational! 

GRAND OPENING 

OPENING PARTY RECAP 

June 27 - June 29, 2025
Let's Refresh: Our NoLita Boutique 
Grand Opening Party! 
We finally got to say “Bonjour, NoLita!” over the last weekend of June. Step inside the Dossier perfume house IRL with us again for the first time. And we pulled out all the stops…a custom engraving station, a live DJ, special events, an ice cream cart for all our guests, etc. Our Dossier Originals room (basically our Creative Lab HQ in NYC) took center stage. Guests finally got to smell all of our scents before they shop…and the line was AROUND the block! Plus, customers had exclusive early access to It Factor, our biggest perfume launch ever. Every shopping trip comes equipped with a bag that says “You smell fantastique.” Our French perfume party? Oui, très chic!
