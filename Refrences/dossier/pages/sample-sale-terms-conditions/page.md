Sample Sale 
The Dossier Sample Sale runs from Friday, May 22, 2026 through Thursday, May 28, 2026. 

1. ELIGIBLE PRODUCTS 
This sale applies to select Dossier products available in the USA. A full list of eligible products is available at checkout. For products not included in the eligible product list, separate discount terms apply as outlined below. 

2. DISCOUNT STRUCTURE 

Dossier + Members : 50 % off all eligible select Dossier products . Discount is automatically applied at checkout , provided customers purchase a minimum of two ( 2 ) eligible sale Dossier products . If fewer than two eligible sale Dossier products are purchased , the sale discount will not apply . This offer applies only to sale items . 

Guest ( non - member ) shoppers : 40 % off all eligible select Dossier products , provided customers purchase a minimum of two ( 2 ) eligible sale Dossier products . available for Guest shoppers on these items .
