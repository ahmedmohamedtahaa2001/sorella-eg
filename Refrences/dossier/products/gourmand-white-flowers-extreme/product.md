About Sweet like sexy, fruity, and floral candy taken to the EXTREME. We’ve up to the ante (and concentration) to create Gourmand White Flowers Extreme. Here are some notes on how we’ve made our bestselling Gourmand White Flowers, inspired by Viktor & Rolf’s Flowerbomb, more intense by upping the fragrance concentration from 18% to 28%. 

Expect more prominent bergamot and berries notes for a bolder fruity and citrus scent at first sniff. The heart features a more intense orange blossom profile to enhance the aroma of fresh flowers–– expect the scent of a lush bouquet as the fragrance settles onto the skin. If you love intensely sensual, edible gourmands––just wait for this fully boosted mélange of warm and rich caramel, vanilla, amber, and patchouli base notes to dry down. It’s pure euphoria in a bottle.
