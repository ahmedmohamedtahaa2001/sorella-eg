About Your happy place, bottled up in a fragrance. Sweet, juicy, and delightfully bright, Orange Glow radiates light, lively, and positive energy. 

The fragrance opens with a delightful fruitiness interlaced with fresh florals over a dynamic, lavish, and velvety base. Spritz yourself into a good mood and gleefully awaken your senses with a floral-coated orange fruit salad––featuring top notes of cantaloupe and orange.

Orange Glow then wraps you in slightly more floral and honeyed heart notes of orange flower and passion fruit. The fragrance dries down to a lustful, sumptuous, and fleshy base of peach and musk notes for scent-sational vibes all day long.
