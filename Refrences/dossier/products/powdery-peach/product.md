About Powdery Peach opens by highlighting sparkling tangerine notes that jostle with the lush smoothness of peach. The fragrance then quickly builds to an opulent bouquet of white flowers, supported by the warmth of vanilla and benzoin.Velvety and vibrant, Powdery Peach is a sensual, almost carnal feminine fragrance, balanced by a colorful and lively fruitiness.
