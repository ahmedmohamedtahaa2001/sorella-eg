About Let’s take fresh masculinity to the EXTREME. We’ve upped the ante (and concentration) to create Aromatic Star Anise Extreme. Here are some notes on how we’ve made our bestselling Aromatic Star Anise, inspired by Dior’s Sauvage, more intense by upping the fragrance concentration from 12% to 25%. 

The bergamot and star anise top notes are boosted to open the scent for a stronger, fresher, and punchier licorice–citrusy scent. We’ve added incense to the heart to add warm, spicy, and earthy depth to the fragrance’s classic “fougere” structure. As the scent dries down, you’ll notice we’ve reinforced the base with more powerful ambery woody notes to enhance the longevity of the fragrance.
