About Smell like a VIP with a modern and feminine scent that’s as easy to wear for a pilates class as a glam night out. It opens with notes of crisp pear and lychee with bedazzling touches of blackcurrant and pink pepper.

At the heart, Dulce Flor evolves into an elegant floral core with a lush bouquet of rose petals and tuberose with sensual jasmine sambac and fresh geranium notes.

All occasion-ready and undeniably uplifting––this fragrance feels clean, vibrant, and beautifully alive, yet with a sensual complexity that deepens over time. The base notes feature a warm blend of vanilla, oakmoss, benzoin, and caramel notes that coat your skin in exquisite richness.

Clean, rich, and irresistible––you’re bound to reach for it every day.
