About Aquatic Vanilla (inspired by Juliette has A Gun's Vanilla Vibes) is the unique fusion of a fresh, salty, marine accord with warm notes of vanilla, musk, benzoin and sandalwood. Orchid and coconut enhance the sunny aura of the fragrance

Contrasted, highly evocative, Aquatic Vanilla (our impression of Juliette has A Gun's Vanilla Vibes) takes orchid and vanilla out of their usual dense character, to bring them a mineral lightness, while recalling the beach and sea scents so dear to our olfactory memories.
