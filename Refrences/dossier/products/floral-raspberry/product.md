About Inspired by Chanel’s Chance Eau Splendide, Floral Raspberry interlaces a laugh, kiss, and an elegant wave into one luminous fruity floral fragrance.

It opens with juicy, effervescent raspberry with supporting blackcurrant and green apple top notes. A sparkling rush of fruity abundance at first sniff.

As the fragrance flourishes on the skin, an airy and elegant bouquet with prominent violet and rose notes unravels at the heart before revealing a creamy, powdery base.

Spritz for lingering lively yet refined femininity, injected with a dose of sweetness and sensuality.
