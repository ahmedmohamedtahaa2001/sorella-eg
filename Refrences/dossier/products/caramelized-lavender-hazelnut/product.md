About Caramelized Lavender & Hazelnut offers a sweet reimagination of lavender, a staple in men’s fragrances, with a salted caramelized hazelnut accord. 

Integrating gourmand notes, more commonly used in women’s fragrances, invites a unisex appeal to the scent. With aromatic lavender and sweet hazelnut, the effect is hypnotizing.
