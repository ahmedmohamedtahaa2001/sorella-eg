About We took notes from a tasty rum and coke to translate the warmth of rum notes with its sweet, almost ambery facets into a fragrance. 

Golden Rum & Amber wraps a natural rum extraction with mellow vanilla and candied fruits for the warm and bubbly feeling of sipping on this beloved happy hour drink.
