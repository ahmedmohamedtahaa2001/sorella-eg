About Spritz yourself with a one-way ticket to your favorite summer escape––basking in the sun or enjoying a cool breeze with a tropical drink in hand. 

Summer Crush transports you to a relaxing, hot day on the beach with refreshing fruity notes of watermelon & lemon with a beach breeze accord. The scent unfolds into a romantic tropical delight with a Ylang Ylang, coconut, and orange blossom heart.

For the base, notes of creamy musks, sensual vanilla, and buttery sandalwood settle on your skin like the most luscious sun tan lotion ––all you’re missing is une paire de lunettes de soleil.
