About Sweet and wholesome yet undeniably desirable, meet the vanilla fragrance that’s an inherent crowd-pleaser. Inspired by Tom Ford’s Vanilla Sex, Gourmand Vanilla opens with a warm, comforting embrace of a prominent, subtly sweet, and buttery almond note interlaced with the warm, spiciness of ginger and milk accord. The fragrance unfolds into a gourmand-kissed bouquet with a heart of coconut, ylang-ylang, jasmine, and orange flower notes that coat the skin in a creamy, sweet, sensual floral aroma. Once well-acquainted with your unique chemistry, Gourmand Vanilla unveils its edible base, starring vanilla sandalwood with caramel and tonka bean supporting notes. Balancing innocent adoration and elegant seductive appeal, Gourmand Vanilla is the beloved fragrance you’ll shamelessly reach for again and again.
