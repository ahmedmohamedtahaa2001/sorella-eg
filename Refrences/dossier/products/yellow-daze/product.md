About Nostalgic banana candy flavors incarnated with more sophisticated notes. 

Yellow Daze refines this lush, ripe, and slightly tart fruity essence for perfume lovers who remain kids at heart. It opens with a burst of ripe banana aroma interlaced with warm, spicy rum, ginger, and zesty grapefruit notes. 

The fragrance matures into an elegant, sumptuous, heart of ylang, jasmine, milky notes, and white woods. Yellow Daze then dries down to a slightly sweet, sensual, earthy, and almost smoky base with notes of vanilla, santal, patchouli, and incense. Smells like a bowl of shapely, fruit-flavored candies, all grown up.
