About I magine a warm and boozy scent that smells like the perfect cognac cocktail. 

Enjoy bitter and distinctive facets of oakwood and Cognac, balanced with praline, candied fruits, coffee, and cinnamon, warmed up with notes of tonka bean and vanilla, for an ideal happy hour or midnight treat. 

Inspired by Kilian’s Angels’ Share, Ambery Oakwood immediately envelops your senses with spicy and warm cordial boozy opening notes of cognac, cinnamon, and candied fruits. The scent then unfolds into a rich, earthy, and smoky heart of oakwood, coffee, and myrrh. Once dried down, Ambery Oakwood coats your skin in a decadent blend of tonka bean, vanilla, and praline notes. Pure cozy, boozy bliss––now available in a spritz.
