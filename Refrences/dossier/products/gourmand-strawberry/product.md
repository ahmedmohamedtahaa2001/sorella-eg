About Gourmand Strawberry (inspired by Juliette Has a Gun’s Miami Shake) is a bright swirl of devourable nostalgia. The fragrance opens with a juicy pop of strawberry blended with warm coconut and a splash of cassis.

Then comes the smooth, creamy center. A milky accord serves up your first sip of strawberry milkshake, folded with soft orange blossom and muguet.

Finally, the fragrance settles into a base of melted vanilla and musks with an unexpected hit of hazelnut.

Sunny, satisfying, and rippled with deliciously-good vibes.
