About Green Fig immediately immerses you in the savor of a juicy fig, enhanced by a bursting green accord . Apple and blackcurrant accompany this flight, which then evolves into more sweetness thanks to tiare flower and dry fruits.

Colorful, lush, Green Fig is like a psychedelic flavor bombing, oscillating between sharp and sweet tones.
