About Creamy, pure, and floral, Milky White is an edible coconut-floral scent with subtly warm, comforting notes. The fragrance opens with a rush of coconut and fresh Lilly of the Valley, adding a sheer lift to the scent. 

Milky White then unfolds with notes of hazelnut, milky accord, and white flowers to coat the skin in a creamy gourmand and elegant floral heart. The fragrance later evolves to reveal base notes of vanilla, white woods, tonka bean, benzoin res, and white musks––slathering the skin with lingering rich vanilla, powdery, woody, and warm aroma. 

Cozy and creamy coconut bliss––this white magic now exists in a spritz.
