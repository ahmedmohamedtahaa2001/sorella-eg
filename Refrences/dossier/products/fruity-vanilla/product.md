About Guilty pleasures meet modern radiance for a devour-worthy fruity vanilla scent with hints of enlivening zest. Fruity Vanilla entices all your senses with subtle, luminous facets coating a heart of candied sweetness and dessert-ready indulgence.

The fragrance opens with crisp, zesty notes of pear, ginger, and mandarin. It then evolves into a sensual, velvety peach heart with jasmine and lavender for a hint of herbal freshness.

Once settled on the skin, the sumptuous vanilla, caramel, and tonka bean base envelops the senses. Decadence––in all its rich, sweet, and sparkling essence––layered in a spritz.
