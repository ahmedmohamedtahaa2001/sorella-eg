About Embodying a sense of spring rebirth and renewal, breathe in the smell of fresh, green, and slightly sweet white spring flowers. Spring Fling captures the essence of the floral freshness and blooming new beginning of the season with renewing floral and revitalizing green notes. 

The fragrance opens with a flourishing burst of fresh, floral, and crisp notes of lily of the valley, green leaves with sweet notes of hyacinth flowers, and a sunny touch of vegan honey. It unfolds into a heart that crafts a lush bouquet of transparent white flowers, magnolia, and sweet pea. 

As it settles on the skin, Spring Fling dries down with a smooth base of narcissus, heliotrope flowers, and musk—the pure joy of spring floral freshness, now in an airy spritz.
