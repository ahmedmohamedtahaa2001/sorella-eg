About Feel the warmth of a sunkissed glow and a comforting embrace in a spritz. Chasing the Sun shines with beachy, fruity, floral, and warm musky notes to inspire the feeling of tanning under the blazing sun while unwinding in the South of France. 

Chasing the Sun glazes your skin with opening top notes of coconut, warm sand accord, bergamot, and plum. The heart notes unfold to drench your skin in a bouquet of orange flower, ylang ylang, tuberose, and jasmin sambac. At the base, warm musks, vanilla, benzoin, myrrh, and patchouli soak into your skin for a sensual gourmand embrace that radiates the summer heat.
