About Craving a life that’s a bowl of cherries? Make it your signature scent with this intoxicating, sophisticated, and fruity fragrance. 

Tantalizing Red offers an expressive—almost hedonic—explosion of cherry aroma with underlying complex and elegant notes. It opens with an assertive burst of cherry, supported by juicy pear and almond notes, before unfolding into a sophisticated floral and woody heart of rose, violet, and sandalwood. 

The fragrance then envelops your skin with a silky, sweet, and earthy delight, featuring base notes of suede, vanilla, musk, benzoin, and cedarwood. It’s a red, luscious, tart, and juicy paradise—bottled in fragrance form.
